# Skill: Market Research

## Description
Conduct targeted market research on a specific topic, competitor, or market segment and produce an actionable intelligence report.

## Trigger
- CMO monthly research sprint
- CEO strategic question
- CPO competitive feature analysis
- CRO technology landscape evaluation

## Input
- Research question or topic
- Scope constraints (geographic, temporal, industry)
- Known competitors or reference points
- Specific data points requested

## Output
- Research report with:
  - Executive summary (3-5 sentences)
  - Market size and growth estimates
  - Key players and their positioning
  - Trends and emerging patterns
  - Opportunities for CoFounderMode
  - Risks and threats
  - Recommended actions
- Source citations for all data points

## Token Cost
MEDIUM — Research is iterative, typically 3K-8K tokens per report.
