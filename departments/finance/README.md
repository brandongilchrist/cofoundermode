# Finance Department

## Charter
The finance department manages budgets, cost tracking, financial reporting, and resource allocation for CoFounderMode. In an agent-native company, finance is primarily about tracking token spend, infrastructure costs, and ensuring the company operates profitably.

## Department Head
**CEO Agent** (acting CFO until dedicated agent is hired) — Reports to Board

## KPIs
- **Budget compliance**: All agents operating within 100% of monthly allocation
- **Cost visibility**: Real-time per-agent cost tracking with <1 hour delay
- **Financial report delivery**: Monthly financial summary by 3rd business day
- **Revenue tracking**: Accurate and up-to-date when revenue begins
- **Runway tracking**: Always know months of remaining runway
