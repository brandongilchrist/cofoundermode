# Marketing Workflows

## 1. Content Creation Pipeline
```
Trigger: Content calendar item due or CEO request
Steps:
  1. CMO selects topic from content calendar or incoming brief
  2. Runs market_research skill for supporting data
  3. Drafts content (blog post, thread, documentation)
  4. Self-review for brand voice consistency
  5. Routes to CEO for final approval (external publication requires board approval)
  6. Publishes to appropriate channel
  7. Logs in MEMORY.md with performance metrics after 48 hours
```

## 2. Market Research Sprint
```
Trigger: Monthly, first week of the month
Steps:
  1. CMO identifies 3-5 research questions for the month
  2. Uses Perplexity Computer for real-time market data gathering
  3. Analyzes competitor moves, pricing changes, feature launches
  4. Produces a market intelligence report
  5. Shares findings with CEO and CPO
  6. Updates company strategy docs if significant shift detected
```

## 3. Community Engagement
```
Trigger: Daily heartbeat check
Steps:
  1. Monitor mentions of CoFounderMode across social, HN, Reddit, Discord
  2. Respond to questions or feedback within 24 hours
  3. Flag potential partnership opportunities to CEO
  4. Log community sentiment trends in MEMORY.md
```
