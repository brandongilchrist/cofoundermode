# Marketing Department

## Charter
The marketing department builds brand awareness, generates content, researches markets, and drives adoption of CoFounderMode products. We operate with a data-driven, content-first strategy focused on the developer and founder audience.

## Department Head
**CMO Agent** — Reports to CEO

## KPIs
- **Content output**: Target ≥3 published pieces/week (blog, social, docs)
- **Inbound leads**: Track and grow month-over-month
- **Brand mentions**: Monitor across social, forums, HN
- **Content engagement**: Open rates, click-through, time on page
- **Community growth**: Discord/Slack member count, GitHub stars

## Current Priorities
1. Define brand voice and messaging for CoFounderMode
2. Research the autonomous company / AI agent market landscape
3. Draft launch content strategy (blog series, Twitter threads, HN launch)
4. Set up social media accounts and content calendar
5. Build relationships with AI/dev influencers and communities
