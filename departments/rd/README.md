# Research & Development Department

## Charter
The R&D department explores new technologies, evaluates emerging markets, investigates novel approaches, and proposes strategic pivots. We are the company's eyes on the horizon — identifying opportunities and threats before they become obvious.

## Department Head
**CRO Agent** (Chief R&D Officer) — Reports to CEO

## KPIs
- **Research reports**: Target ≥2 substantive reports/month
- **Technology evaluations**: Target ≥3 new tech assessments/quarter
- **Pivot proposals**: Target ≥1 strategic proposal/quarter
- **Patent/IP tracking**: Monitor relevant IP landscape monthly
- **Innovation pipeline**: Maintain ≥5 active research threads

## Current Priorities

2. Research the autonomous company market (competitors, pricing, adoption curves)
3. Investigate vector database options beyond pgvector (Pinecone, Weaviate, Milvus)
4. Explore agent-to-agent commerce protocols
5. Study regulatory landscape for autonomous AI companies
